Burger Recipe

Hello and welcome. Are you interested in making the perfect burger for the barbecue? Well, you've come to the right place!

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 small onion, grated
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 lb medium ground beef

Now that you have your ingredients, it's time to make your burger!

Directions:

  • Lightly oil grill
  • Whisk egg in a bowl, then add the next 6 ingredients
  • Using your hands, crumble the beef and mix together
  • Gently shape mixture into burgers about 3/4 inch thick
  • To make sure that the burger doesn't puff in the middle, press your thumb down in the center of the burger
  • Place burger on grill for 6-8 minutes on each side
  • Cook until at least 160F
